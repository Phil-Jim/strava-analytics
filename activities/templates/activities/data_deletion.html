Data Deletion Instructions

For Facebook Users

If you logged in using Facebook and wish to delete your data:

Automatic Deletion

When you delete our app from your Facebook account, all your data associated with this application will be automatically removed from our systems within 30 days.

Manual Deletion Request

To request immediate deletion of your data:

  1. Email us at: privacy@stravaanalytics.com
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your Facebook user ID or email address used to log in
  4. We will process your request within 48 hours

What Data We Delete

  • Your Facebook profile information
  • Your Strava activity data
  • All analytics and cached data
  • Any preferences or settings

Data We Cannot Delete

We may retain aggregated, anonymized data for analytics purposes that cannot be linked back to you.
